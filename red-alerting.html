Red Alerting System

A real-time alerting system for healthcare providers to notify about critical errors or emergencies. Ensures timely intervention and safety.

How the System Works

This system is designed to send notifications in real-time to healthcare professionals when critical errors or emergency situations occur, allowing for a faster response and minimizing the risks associated with medical emergencies.

Key Features

  • Instant Notification: Alerts are sent immediately to healthcare providers, including doctors, nurses, and staff.
  • Multi-Channel Alerts: Notifications can be delivered via various methods such as email, SMS, and mobile apps.
  • High Priority: Critical issues are flagged with high priority, ensuring quick attention from medical professionals.
  • Integration with Patient Data: The alerting system is integrated with patient records and medical devices to trigger alerts based on real-time data.
